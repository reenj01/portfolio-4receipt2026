Navigating London Through Street Graphics & Design

Throughout my three weeks in London and exploring London's creative energy at UAL, I explored how London can be navigated not by map directions, but the designs and graphics that are existing in the streets.


Using physical materials, I created a 16.5 in x 23.2 in collage map, and added NFC tags behind each of the journies from one point to another. When a person taps their phone near the wireless symbol, a website opens up and allows them to embark on a graphic visualization of that specific journey around London.
