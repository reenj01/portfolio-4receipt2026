LumiNoto

How might we reduce the chance of losing fleeting thoughts, and better organize jumbled ideas through a more natural integration of AI interaction?


I owned the full design process from initial concept, user research, to initial prototype. I began with research into how people capture, organize, and revisit notes across physical and digital mediums, then translated those findings into explicit user journeys, task flows, and interaction patterns that reduced friction while increasing effectiveness. Throughout, I treated human-computer interaction principles as the backbone of the work—carefully designing feedback, affordances, and information hierarchy so that LumiNoto feels intuitive on first use yet powerful over time.


Year

Fall 2025

Tools
