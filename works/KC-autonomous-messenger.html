K.C. Autonomous Messenger

This speculative design work shows the switch between the power dynamics of humans and AI robots. Humans no longer have power over robots and cannot command it to do certain things for their own benefits, like how they would use a tool. Like two individual people, AI robots and humans are able to perform two way conversations—sometimes even a one way with the robot being the main. They have their own generated thoughts and can respond to the user however they want without necessarily following the user's desires.
