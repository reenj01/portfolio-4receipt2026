Collaborative Mood Light

The keyword in this work is "collaborative" for it needs more than one person to interact with the work in order to gain the full experience.


The user needs to flip the symmetrical cone handle which would open three small doors on the edges of the light container. Inside those small doors will be three force sensitive resistors (FSR) that the users can press. Since there are three FSRs, more than one person will be required to fully control all three inputs at the same time. Each sensor corresponds to each of the RGB colors—red, green, and blue. The led light would light up in the corresponding power of the pressure that the user puts onto each sensor. The harder they press, the brighter the light. As the users press more than only sensor at a time, the RGB colors will mix together to create beautiful shades and hues of colors.
